What is Gadfly?

Gadfly is an open source civic transparency tool built to show everdday Americans if their congressional representatives actually represent them

How it works

Enter your zip code and Gadfly finds your House representative and two senators. For each representative, we analyze their voting records across 11 policy categories selected for their impact on the daily lives of most Americans. This like healthcare, housing, individual rights, and government accountability. Each bill for the 119th Congress is analyzed using AI to determine which categoreis it touches and which directions it moves

Carrots & Sticks

We highlight both the good and the bad. The legislation pages show where a representative sponsored, cosponsored, or voted for bills that raised red flags, and where they stood up for transparency, individual rights, and their constituents' interests. The goal is simple: give represenatives an incentive to actually who elected them.

Open Source

Gadfly is an open source project. Contributions, data sharing, and feature suggestions are welcome.

Method Behind the Madness

  • House Voting Data Obtained from Congress Api - Congress API
  • Senate Voting Data Obtained from - This feature is coming in the future
  • Member & Vote Data — All congressional member information and voting records are pulled directly from the official Congress.gov API and stored locally so the app can quickly look up your representatives and their votes.
  • AI Analysis — The full text of each bill is sent to Claude, Anthropic's AI, along with a detailed analysis prompt. Claude reads the bill and returns a structured breakdown of which categories it touches, which direction it moves, and whether any red flags are present.
  • Temperature set to 0 — Claude gives the most consistent, deterministic answer possible. No creativity, no randomness.
  • Independent analysis — Each bill is analyzed against 11 categories and 9 flags independently. Claude doesn't know how other bills were scored.
  • Large bill handling — Bills too large to process in one call are split into sections, analyzed separately, and synthesized into a single result.
  • Long term goal — Future Feature: A user-friendly database giving citizens direct access to every vote, bill text, and flag — fully searchable.
  • Human verification — Future Feature: A rigorous review process where verified human checks confirm AI categorizations, with a checkmark displayed next to verified votes.
